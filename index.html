Personal Projects

Ball Balancing Robot in Unity

This project simulates a ball balancing robot in Unity. The robot keeps the ball centered on a platform using PID (Proportional-Integral-Derivative) control. Unity's joint functions were employed to manage the platform's movement, ensuring stability and precision in the simulation.

Space Movement

Inspired by popular titles like War Thunder and Gundam, I embarked on a personal project to create a realistic space warfare game. The primary goal of this project was to develop an immersive and engaging space combat experience that balanced realism with accessible gameplay. A key challenge that I encountered during the development process was creating smooth and intuitive controls for the player's spacecraft within the context of a three-dimensional space environment, which included additional axes of movement beyond the standard X, Y, and Z coordinates.

Convai

I embarked on a journey to integrate conversational AI into my game development endeavors. The primary objective was to create an immersive escape room experience where players could engage not only with the game environment and objects but also with AI characters through natural language interactions. The AI was designed to simulate human-like conversations, respond to the player's queries, interact with other AI entities, and assist in solving puzzles within the escape room.
